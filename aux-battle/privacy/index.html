Aux Battle

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Aux Battle is a multiplayer music party game built by Westfall Group. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what we do not do.

What We Collect

Aux Battle collects the minimum information needed to run the game:

  • Player name — the name you enter when creating or joining a room. This is visible to other players in your game session and is stored temporarily in Firestore while the session is active.
  • Anonymous user ID — a randomly generated identifier created by Firebase Authentication. It is not linked to your Apple ID, email address, phone number, or any other personal account. It exists only to keep you in the correct game room.
  • Gameplay data — your song selections, scores, and round results within a game session. This data is stored in Firestore and automatically deleted after 24 hours via a TTL policy.

What We Do Not Collect

  • Email address, phone number, or any account credentials
  • Location data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access
  • Apple Music account details — music playback is handled entirely by Apple's MusicKit framework and never passes through our servers
  • Device advertising identifiers (IDFA)

Analytics & Tracking

Aux Battle does not use any analytics SDKs, crash reporting services, or third-party tracking tools. We do not track you across other apps or websites.

Advertising

Aux Battle does not display ads and does not share any data with advertising networks.

In-App Purchases

Aux Battle offers an optional one-time in-app purchase to unlock additional content packs. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We never see or store your payment information.

Data Storage & Retention

Gameplay data (player names, song picks, scores) is stored in Google Firebase Firestore and is automatically deleted within 24 hours of a session ending. No gameplay data is retained long-term.

Your player name is saved locally on your device so you don't have to retype it. You can clear it at any time by deleting the app.

Children's Privacy

Aux Battle is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Third-Party Services

Aux Battle uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase — real-time database and anonymous authentication
  • Apple MusicKit — music search and playback (optional)
  • iTunes Search API — song search for users without Apple Music

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of Aux Battle after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
